# MIT 8.04 Quantum Mechanics I — Lecture Notes
Open-source lecture notes for [MIT OCW 8.04 Quantum Mechanics I](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/8-04-quantum-physics-i-spring-2016/) (Prof. Barton Zwiebach, 2016).
**Author:** Daniel Wang  
**Advised by:** Dr. Dimitri Dounas-Frazer (Lakeside School)  
**Compiled:** Fall 2024 – May 2025 · **155 pages**
[Download PDF](./8_04_Quantum_Mechanics_I.pdf)
## Contents
Notes follow the OCW lecture structure, covering:
1. Background  
2. Matter waves  
3. Operators & the Schrödinger equation  
4. The wavefunction  
5. Fourier analysis  
6. Expectation values & eigenstates  
7. Solving the Schrödinger equation  
8. General properties  
9. Harmonic oscillator  
10. Scattering states  
11. Scattering theory  
12. Central potentials  
13. Hydrogen atom  
14. Toward matrix mechanics
